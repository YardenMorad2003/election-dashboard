Privacy Policy

Israeli Elections MCP server · Effective 10 July 2026

This policy covers the Israeli Elections connector — an MCP (Model Context Protocol) server at israeli-elections-mcp.yardenmorad2003.workers.dev that answers queries about published Israeli national election results (1992–2022), vote-transfer estimates, and public socio-economic statistics. It is operated by Yarden Morad, an independent developer.

What data we collect

None. The service:

  • has no user accounts, sign-in, or authentication;
  • sets no cookies and embeds no trackers or analytics;
  • does not store, log, or retain the content of your queries — each request is processed in memory to produce a response and then discarded;
  • never sees your conversation. Only the specific tool call (e.g. a locality name and election number) reaches the server, sent by your AI assistant.

What data the service returns

Only public, aggregate, historical data: official Central Elections Committee results by locality, Israel National Election Studies survey aggregates, and CBS municipal statistics. The dataset contains no personal data about any voter.

Infrastructure providers

Requests are served by Cloudflare Workers, and the underlying dataset is fetched from GitHub Pages. As network operators, these providers may process transient technical metadata (such as IP addresses and request counts) to deliver and secure the service, under their own privacy policies. We do not receive, store, or use that metadata.

Your AI assistant

Your conversation with ChatGPT, Claude, or any other client is governed by that provider's privacy policy (e.g. OpenAI or Anthropic), not by this one. This service only receives the individual tool calls the assistant chooses to send.

Control and deletion

Because we store nothing, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. Disconnecting the connector in your assistant's settings stops all requests to the service.

Changes

Any change to this policy will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

Contact

Questions or concerns: open an issue at github.com/YardenMorad2003/election-dashboard/issues — see also the support page.
